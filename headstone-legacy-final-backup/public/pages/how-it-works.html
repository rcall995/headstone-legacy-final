How It Works

Step 1: Choose Your Tier

Select a memorial plan that best suits your needs, from a simple, elegant tribute to a comprehensive digital legacy connecting multiple family members. Each tier is designed to preserve a life story in a meaningful way.

Step 2: Create the Memorial

Using our intuitive form, you can add personal details, write a biography, upload cherished photos and videos, and record important life milestones. For our Historian tier, you can also add cemetery locations and link to other family memorials.

Step 3: Share the Legacy

Once created, each memorial page has a unique QR code. This code can be printed or attached to a headstone, allowing any visitor with a smartphone to instantly access the rich, digital tribute you've built, connecting past, present, and future generations.
