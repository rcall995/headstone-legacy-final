How It Works

1. Create the Story

Build a beautiful, permanent tribute by adding a biography, cherished photos, life milestones, and important dates, bringing their unique story to life.

2. Link with QR Code

Every memorial gets a unique QR code. This code can be etched onto a plaque and attached to the physical headstone, creating a bridge to the digital tribute.

3. Share the Legacy

Any visitor can simply scan the QR code with their smartphone to instantly access the rich memorial page, connecting with the life story you have preserved.
